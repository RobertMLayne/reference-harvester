Improving the USPTO's digital space

The USPTO is committed to continuously improving the customer experience on our web platforms to enhance and modernize accessibility, design, and overall satisfaction in our digital space. Below are some of the recent improvements we have made to the agency's digital spaces. 

Customer experience initiatives

  • Improving the experience for trademark customers
  • Plain language at the USPTO
  • Accessibility of the USPTO facilities, website, and files
